# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community experience for
contributors and users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate intimidation or exclusion.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to project maintainers through the security
or maintainer contact channels documented in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or reject comments, issues, or contributions that violate
this policy, and may restrict participation when necessary.
